The AI Work Passport Company

The next enterprise credential is not the resume. It is proof that a human can safely operate digital labor. We license humans to work with AI agents — in specific work contexts, at specific autonomy levels.

Who is ready to work with AI agents? Praxis turns that question from a guess into evidence.
zone 1 · the shift

The 2028 workforce transition

① Software User

2015–2020. Employees used software tools to execute work manually.

② AI Assistant User

2020–2025. Employees prompted AI assistants to accelerate tasks.

③ AI Workflow Operator

2025+. Employees direct, verify, constrain, and escalate AI-agent work.

the new class of worker is the AI-native operator. employers need a system of record for that capability.

zone 2 · the risk

Enterprises can't tell who is safe

Recruiting

A recruiter touches candidate data via AI pipelines. One bad decision cascades.

Finance

An analyst uses AI in financial reports. Unchecked outputs create compliance risk.

Customer Comms

A sales rep sends external messages via AI. Unverified content reaches clients.

Operations

An ops user updates core systems via AI. Unvalidated changes propagate downstream.

the sharper risk: enterprises won't know who can be trusted with which AI workflow.
